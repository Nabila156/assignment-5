Important FAQ

What is the purpose of the donation website?

The donation website aims to raise funds to provide immediate relief to victims affected by the recent floods and to support individuals injured during the quota movement in Bangladesh. It seeks to ensure that essential resources such as food, medical supplies, and shelter are delivered to those in need.

How can I donate?

You can donate by visiting our website, where you will find various payment options, including bank transfers, mobile payments, and online payment gateways. Simply select your preferred method, enter the donation amount, and follow the instructions to complete your contribution.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations made through our website may be tax-deductible, depending on your country's tax laws. We recommend consulting with a tax professional to understand the specifics related to your donation.

How will the funds be used?

All donations will be allocated transparently to cover essential needs such as food, clean water, medical care, rehabilitation for the injured, and long-term recovery efforts for flood victims. We will provide regular updates on how the funds are being utilized.

Can I volunteer or help in other ways?

Absolutely! Besides monetary donations, we welcome volunteers to help with fundraising, awareness campaigns, and distribution of relief supplies. You can sign up through our website to get involved in various initiatives supporting affected communities.
